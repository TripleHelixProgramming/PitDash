# Team Detail

[← Back to README](../../README.md)

Everything you'd want to know about a single team — reached by tapping any team card, ranking row, pit-map cell, or schedule entry.

- **TBA summary** at the top — RP per match, OPR, DPR, CCWM, and average score for the current event.
- **Match history** is the headline feature: every match the team has played at the event, most recent first, with the qualification/playoff number, win/loss result, final score (their alliance vs. opponents), and the six avatars for that match so you can see who they played with and against.
- Color-coded `W` / `L` makes performance trends easy to scan at a glance.
- **Tap any match row** to open the full breakdown or results for that match, along with a link to the match video on YouTube when one is available.
