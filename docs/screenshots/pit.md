# Pit View

[← Back to README](../../README.md)

The home screen. Everything a driver or coach wants at a glance.

- **Status banner** (`NOW QUEUING` in this screenshot) — live queuing state from Nexus: `QUEUING SOON` → `NOW QUEUING` → `ON DECK` → `ON FIELD`.
- **Countdown** — time until your next match. The background color reflects the **alliance color you're assigned to for that match** (blue or red), so a glance across the pit tells you which side you're on. Labeled `Nexus estimated` when real field timing is available, with data-source dots (Nexus / TBA / Statbotics) showing which feeds are currently reachable.
- **Next-up strip** — what's playing now and the clock time of your next match.
- **Win-probability bar** and score prediction (Statbotics) comparing the two alliances.
- **Alliance cards** — both alliances for your upcoming match with team avatar, record, current ranking (e.g. `R18`), pit location (e.g. `C10`), and per-team EPA / Auto / Teleop / Endgame breakdown. **Tap any card to open that team's full details** (match history, awards, event performance, etc.).
