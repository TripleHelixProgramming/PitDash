# Robot

[← Back to README](../../README.md)

A team-configurable view backed by a spreadsheet — whatever your team puts in it shows up here, so the Robot tab can be whatever reference material is most useful at the pit.

Discrete sections of the spreadsheet become **separate expansion tiles** that collapse and expand independently, so the page stays scannable no matter how much data you load in. The screenshot shows the `PDP` tile expanded; other tiles (not visible here) sit below it and open on tap.

In this example the team has loaded their **power-distribution wiring** — channel, breaker, controller type, CAN ID/bus, subsystem — which is handy for pit-crew diagnostics and repairs. Other teams use it for checklists, setpoints, auto routines, pit schedules, or anything else they'd otherwise keep on a clipboard.
