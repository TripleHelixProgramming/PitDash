# Pit Map

[← Back to README](../../README.md)

A quick reference for finding any team at the event. Pit locations come from Nexus.

- Teams are arranged by their actual pit address (e.g. `A16`, `C1`) so the layout on screen roughly mirrors the venue.
- **Pinch to zoom in and out**, and **drag to pan** around the hall — the map is fully interactive, not a static image.
- **Mini-map** in the lower right shows where your current view sits within the whole venue; use it to jump across the map without scrolling cell by cell.
- Your team's pit is highlighted, and the **alliance partners and opponents for your upcoming match are highlighted too**, so you can find everyone you need to talk to before queuing.
- Tap a team cell to jump to their details.
